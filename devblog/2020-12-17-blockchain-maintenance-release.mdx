The `2020.12.16.0` release is a minor blockchain maintenance release. The primary goal of this
release is to bump the blessed snapshot.

<!--truncate-->

#### Blessed Snapshot Update

This release bumps the blessed snapshot to height `635041` which will improve sync times of newly
onboarded Hotspots.

### Plan

We began beta-testing `2020.12.16.0` around 9:00 PM PT, December 16, 2020 and plan to GA on
Thursday, December 17, 2020 around 11:00 AM PST.
