The Nova developers have tagged a new `2022.04.14.0` firmware release for Original Helium Hotspots.
This minor release increases the interval a Hotspot checks for a new `miner` container from 4 hours
(6 times a day) to 24 hours (once per day).

<!--truncate-->

### Plan

We have been testing `2022.04.14.0` since Apr 14, 2022 11:00 AM PT. Current ETA for GA is Apr 14,
2022 1:00 PM PT.
