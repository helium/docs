# Tago.IO

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl'
import LegacyContentBanner from '@site/src/theme/LegacyContentBanner'

<LegacyContentBanner />

[Tago.IO](https://tago.io/) combines advanced device management, services, data storage,
visualization, and analytics with an easy to implement cloud-based IoT platform.

You can sign up for a free account at [https://tago.io](https://tago.io/).

To get started, log in to your Tago.IO account and click **Devices** from the menu on the left:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-tago-data-plugins03.png')} />

Generate a new **Authorization Token** by entering any name on the **Token name** field and click
the **Generate token** button.

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-tago-generate-token.png')} />

Now we simply copy the **Authorization Token** by clicking on the **Copy** icon:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-tago-copy-token.png')} />

At this point, we move back over to the Helium Console to complete the Integration.

Click on **Integrations** on the menu on the left and select the **Tago.IO** Prebuilt Integration:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-tago-integrations.png')} />

Paste the **Tago.IO Authorization Token** that we copied from the Tago.IO site above and enter a
name for our Integration:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-tago-integrations-create.png')} />

Click **Add Integration**. You should now see a verification message that the Integration was
created successfully:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-tago-integration-success.png')} />

## Connecting Integrations to Devices

Devices or groups of devices (via labels) can be directly connected to integrations on the Flows
Worskpace. Labels are identifiers used to group devices for easy management. To connect one or more
devices to one or more integrations, simply connect the Device and Integration nodes on the Flows
Workspace.

:::info

Quick video tutorial connecting devices to an integration
[here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnERw1f7TGE).

:::

Node elements (devices, labels, integrations) need to be created before they're available on the
Flows Workspace. More information about Flows [here](/console/flows/).
