# Ubidots

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl'
import LegacyContentBanner from '@site/src/theme/LegacyContentBanner'

<LegacyContentBanner />

[Ubidots](https://ubidots.com/) allows you to rapidly assemble and launch Internet of Things (IoT)
applications without having to write code or hire a software development team. You can sign up for a
free account at [https://ubidots.com](https://ubidots.com/).

:::tip

Additional information can be found on the Ubidots user guide to
[Connect Helium with Ubidots](https://help.ubidots.com/en/articles/5008195-plugins-connect-helium-with-ubidots)

:::

To get started, log in to your Ubidots account and select **Plugins** from the **Devices** dropdown
menu at the top of your screen:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins01.png')} />

Click the **Create Data Plugin** button:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins02.png')} />

Select **Helium**:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins03.png')} />

Click the **>** (forward) button:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins04.png')} />

Under **Ubidots Token**, select the **Token** you wish to associate with this integration (we will
just use **Default token**) and make sure **Device tags** is selected under **Use Helium labels
as**:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins05.png')} />

Now, simply click the checkbox button:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins06.png')} />

You should get a confirmation message:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins07.png')} />

And see your new Data Plugin being configured:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins08.png')} />

Now, click your avatar in the upper-right of your screen and select **API Credentials**:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins09.png')} />

Click the **Clipboard** button to the right of **Default token** to copy it:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins10.png')} />

Now, let's move over to the **Helium Console** and create our **Integration**. Select
**Integrations** from the menu on the left of the screen:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console01.png')} />

And click **Ubidots** under **Add a Prebuilt Integration**:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console02.png')} />

Now, under **Enter Auth Token**, paste the Ubidots Default token we copied and click the **Get
Webhook URL** button:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console03.png')} />

You should now get another confirmation message:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console04.png')} />

And see the **Ubidots Webhook URL**:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console05.png')} />

Now, we simply name our Integration:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console06.png')} />

After clicking on the **Add Integration** button, we can review the details:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console08.png')} />

Notice that currently, no devices are piped through this Integration:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console09.png')} />

Let's fix that!

## Connecting Integrations to Devices

Devices or groups of devices (via labels) can be directly connected to integrations on the Flows
Worskpace. Labels are identifiers used to group devices for easy management. To connect one or more
devices to one or more integrations, simply connect the Device and Integration nodes on the Flows
Workspace.

:::info

Quick video tutorial connecting devices to an integration
[here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnERw1f7TGE).

:::

Node elements (devices, labels, integrations) need to be created before they're available on the
Flows Workspace. More information about Flows [here](/console/flows/).

Click on **Devices** on the menu on the left of your screen.

And open up a device that we want to send through to Ubidots.

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console11.png')} />

At this point, we should be good to go! We can click on the **Debug** button on the right of the
Device screen and wait for the data to start coming through:

<img src={useBaseUrl('img/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console16.png')} />
