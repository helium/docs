## EMB-LR1280S

The new LoRa® 2.4 Ghz Embit solution to connect your sensors and actuators leveraging both the
advantages of LoRa® long range modulation and 2.4 Ghz RF properties and regulation. It offers a wide
range of serial intefaces, a crypto unit and extremely low consumptions. It represents the perfect
solution for many industrial applications with the aim of ensuring worldwide deployment and enhanced
transmission rate.
