The Helium Network supports any LoRaWAN capable device meeting the v1.0.2, v1.0.3, or v1.0.4
specification.

:::info

There are thousands of compatible LoRaWAN devices. The devices shown here do not represent all
available devices.

:::

### Complete Device Solutions

Please visit Users under our [Helium Ecosystem](https://www.helium.com/ecosystem) catalog for
companies offering complete end-to-end device solutions.
