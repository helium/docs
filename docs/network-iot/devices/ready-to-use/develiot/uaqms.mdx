## Urban Air Quality Monitoring Station

The Urban Air Quality Monitoring Station measures the key air quality indicators and environmental
parameters with high accuracy, mapping the pollution in real-time.

### Specifications

#### Sensors:

- air quality options: CO, NO2, SO2, O3, NH3, H2S, Cl2, HCl, HF, CH2O, H2
- particulate matter (PM)
- low concentration CO2
- GPS
- temperature
- humidity
- barometer
- noise level
- wind direction
- wind speed
- precipitation
- battery

#### Dimensions:

- width: 325 mm
- length: 430 mm
- height: 185 mm

#### Weight:

- 11000 grams

#### Battery:

- type: Lead Acid
- replaceable: True
