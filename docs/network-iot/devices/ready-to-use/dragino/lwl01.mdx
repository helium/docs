## LWL01

LWL01 -- LoRaWAN Water Leak Sensor

The Dragino LWL01 is a LoRaWAN Water Leak Sensor. It detects the water leak status. The Dragino
LWL01 will send periodic data every day as well as for water leak events. It also counts the water
leak event times and also calculates the last water leak duration.
