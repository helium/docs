## enginko EGK-LW22PLG LoRaWAN Energy Meter Plug with On/Off

EGK-LW22PLG LoRaWAN Energy Meter Plug with On/Off

The EGK-LW22PLG is a compact C-class LoRaWAN energy meter that can monitor and switch a 230 V AC -
16 Amp Load (active, reactive and apparent energy, Class 0.2s), equipped with a rechargeable battery
for mains outage detection, with configurable default power-on status.

#### Dimensions:

#### Weight:

#### Note:
