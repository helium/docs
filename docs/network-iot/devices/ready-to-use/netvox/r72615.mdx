## Wireless Outdoor CO2/Temperature/Humidity Sensor with Solar Panel

The R72615 is equipped with a temperature and humidity sensor that detects and transmits ambient
temperature and humidity data. It is a wireless communication method that uses the SX1276 wireless
communication module. The R72615 has a CO2 sensor that detects the concentration of CO2 in the air.
