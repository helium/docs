## Wireless CO2/Temperature/Humidity Sensor

RA0715 is equipped with RA0715. It can monitor the ambient temperature and humidity and send the
data via wireless method that adopts SX1276 wireless communication module. RA0715 is equipped with
CO2 sensor that can detect the concentration of CO2 in the air.
