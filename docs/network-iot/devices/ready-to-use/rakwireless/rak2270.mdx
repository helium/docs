## Overview

RAK2270 is a state-of-the-art sticker tracker designed for the modern needs of LoRaWAN®
applications. This sleek, label-like device effortlessly attaches to any product, offering an
elegant solution for various tracking requirements. Whether you're a seasoned developer, an IoT
platform manager, or a solutions provider, the RAK2270 is engineered to meet your specific needs.

RAK2270 supports motion detection, shock detection, temperature monitoring, reading retrieval and
comes with adhesives so you can easily apply it on your package.

RAK2270 design is also open source, and readily accessible on
[their repository](https://github.com/RAKWireless/RAK2270-Sticker-Tracker).

### Product Features

- WisDuo Module RAK3172-SiP/RAK3172LP-SiP
- STM32WL SoC
- Built-in 3-axis accelerometer
- Temperature monitoring with a range of -20° C to +60° C and a resolution of 0.1° C
- Built-in battery: LiMnO2 400 mAh
- Small size: 85x118x1.5 mm
