## SenseCAP Wireless Soil Moisture and Temperature Sensor - LoRaWAN®

It measures soil volumetric water content (VWC) and temperature. It's designed with a 2-in-1 sensor
that integrates soil temperature and soil moisture, a custom battery and an IP66 enclosure,
optimized for outdoor use cases that need reliable data collection over years.
