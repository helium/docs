## Sensecap T1000 Tracker

SenseCAP T1000 Tracker, as compact as a credit card, effortlessly fits into a pocket or attaches to
an asset. It's equipped with GPS, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi for seamless indoor and outdoor positioning.
Beyond just location tracking, it observes parameters like temperature, brightness, and movement.
With an emergency button and buzzer, and a battery life that stretches for months on a single
charge, it assures reliable tracking across various applications with an IP65 rating.
