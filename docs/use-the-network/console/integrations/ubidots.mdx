# Ubidots

[Ubidots](https://ubidots.com/) allows you to rapidly assemble and launch
Internet of Things (IoT) applications without having to write code or hire
a software development team. You can sign up for a free
account at [https://ubidots.com](https://ubidots.com/).

To get started, log in to your Ubidots account and select **Plugins** from the
**Devices** dropdown menu at the top of your screen:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins01.png"
  )}
/>

Click the **Create Data Plugin** button:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins02.png"
  )}
/>

Select **Helium**:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins03.png"
  )}
/>

Click the **>** (forward) button:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins04.png"
  )}
/>

Under **Ubidots Token**, select the **Token** you wish to associate with this integration
(we will just use **Default token**) and make sure **Device tags** is selected under
**Use Helium labels as**:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins05.png"
  )}
/>

Now, simply click the checkbox button:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins06.png"
  )}
/>

You should get a confirmation message:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins07.png"
  )}
/>

And see your new Data Plugin being configured:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins08.png"
  )}
/>

Now, click your avatar in the upper-right of your screen and select **API Credentials**:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins09.png"
  )}
/>

Click the **Clipboard** button to the right of **Default token** to copy it:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-data-plugins10.png"
  )}
/>

Now, let's move over to the **Helium Console** and create our **Integration**.
Select **Integrations** from the menu on the left of the screen:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console01.png"
  )}
/>

And click **Ubidots** under **Add a Prebuilt Integration**:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console02.png"
  )}
/>

Now, under **Enter Auth Token**, paste the Ubidots Default token we copied and click
the **Get Webhook URL** button:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console03.png"
  )}
/>

You should now get another confirmation message:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console04.png"
  )}
/>

And see the **Ubidots Webhook URL**:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console05.png"
  )}
/>

Now, we simply name our Integration:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console06.png"
  )}
/>

And add a label to the Integration:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console07.png"
  )}
/>

After clicking on the **Add Integration** button, we can review the details:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console08.png"
  )}
/>

Notice that currently, no devices are piped through this Integration:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console09.png"
  )}
/>

Let's fix that!

Click on **Devices** on the menu on the left of your screen:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console10.png"
  )}
/>

And open up a device that we want to send through to Ubidots:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console11.png"
  )}
/>

Click the **+ Add Label** button and select the label that we created above:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console12.png"
  )}
/>

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console13.png"
  )}
/>

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console14.png"
  )}
/>

We now can see that the label with the Ubidots Integration is now bound to this device:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console15.png"
  )}
/>

At this point, we should be good to go! We can click on the **Debug** button on
the right of the Device screen and wait for the data to start coming through:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl(
    "img/use-the-network/console/integrations/integrations-ubidots-helium-console16.png"
  )}
/>
