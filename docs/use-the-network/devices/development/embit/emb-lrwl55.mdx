## EMB-LRWL55

EMB-LRWL55 is the first ultra-low-power LoRaWAN速 OEM module based on STM's new
system-on-chip, STM32WL55xx. Its tiny form factor and its capability of working
both with 868MHz EU ISM band and 915MHz US make EMB-LRWL55 the perfect solution
for small and battery powered LoRaWAN速 end-devices, with the possibility to
interface any kind of sensors.
