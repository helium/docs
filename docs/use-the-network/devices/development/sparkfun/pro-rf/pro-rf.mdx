## Introduction

The SparkFun Pro RF is a LoRaÂ®-enabled wireless board that marries a SAMD21 and
a long-range RFM95W to make a compact and easy-to-use IoT Arduino board. With
its fast MCU and excellent point to point data transmission in the 915MHz ISM
band with LoRa Capabilities, the Pro RF is a great choice for anyone interested
or experienced in utilizing long range data communication.
