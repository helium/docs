## Abeeway Smart Badge

Portable multi-mode tracker in an ID card format. This device is ideal for tracking, zone
notification and monitoring of workforce safety and security. Simple to use, a single button gives
you access to the numerous functionalities you may personalize for your needs, e.g., as a Panic/SOS
Button. A local zoning feature is able to signal danger zones with an 80db buzzer.
