## BoatOfficer Blue

With BoatOfficer Blue you can monitor your boat, while you’re not around. E.g. position, battery
voltage and temperature are send via TheThingsNetwork to the BoatOfficer app on your phone. Events
like water ingress detection trigger an immediate push notification.
