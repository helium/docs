## MClimate Vicki - Smart Radiator Thermostat

MClimate Vicki is a a smart thermostatic radiator valve (TRV). It controls residential and
commercial hot water radiators, measures temperature and humidity and the user can adjust the target
temperature by the LEDs screen and rotating the exterior of the device.
